Why Study Systems Matter

Without a system, studying becomes chaotic. You might cram for exams, forget what you learned, and repeat the cycle. A good study system turns learning into a habit.

Key Components of an Effective System

  • Active recall techniques
  • Spaced repetition
  • Consistent review schedules

Implement these, and you'll see real improvement in retention and understanding.
