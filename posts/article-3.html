The Feynman Technique: Learn Anything Faster by Teaching It

Richard Feynman's learning method is deceptively simple — and it works for everything from physics to programming to literature.

The Feynman Technique is a powerful way to deepen your understanding of any topic by explaining it as if teaching someone else.

How to Apply the Technique

  1. Choose a concept
  2. Explain it simply
  3. Identify gaps in your understanding
  4. Review and simplify further

This method reveals what you truly know and what needs more work.
